# Noise Robustness Results

This page reports the impact of **noisy knowledge** on the model's base-prediction confidence.

- Values are reported as **Median [Q1–Q3]**
- **Post@10**, **Post@20**, and **Post@30** refer to injecting 10, 20, and 30 random noisy statements
- Across models, the largest confidence drop typically appears after the first batch of noise, with smaller changes afterward
